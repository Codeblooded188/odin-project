Rice Recipe

Description

Rice is a versatile and staple food enjoyed in various cultures around the world. It's prepared by cooking rice grains until they become fluffy and tender. Whether served as a side or a main dish, rice can be paired with a wide range of ingredients, such as vegetables, meats, seafood, and sauces. From the fragrant saffron-infused Basmati rice to the savory flavors of fried rice, rice dishes offer a delightful combination of textures and tastes that cater to different culinary preferences.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of rice
  • 4 cups of water
  • 1 tablespoon of salt

Steps

  1. Combine the rice and water in a pot.
  2. Bring the mixture to a boil.
  3. Reduce the heat and simmer for 15 minutes.
